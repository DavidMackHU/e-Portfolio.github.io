Society of Collegiate Black Men

The Society of collegiate black men is a sacred fraternal organization dedicated to uniting its membership and promoting constructive relationships among black men. Through educational, extracurricular, and service-based activities, The society facilitates the development of the positive black male role model On Howard University's campus and in communities abroad. Joining SCBM will give you connection and leadership skills that can be applied to any major.

Eagle Scout - Boy Scouts

Achieving the rank of Eagle Scout in the Boy Scouts has been one of my proudest accomplishments. It reflects my dedication to leadership, community service, and personal development.
